Our Campus

Welcome! In addition to two main campuses — Fort Garry and Bannatyne — the University of Manitoba has multiple satellite sites throughout the province and research settings around the world. The University features new, modern facilities complemented by historic buildings that have defined the campus for more than a century. These innovative spaces enrich the experiences of students, our community and visitors to campus.

About Us

We attract people from around the world who share our ideals and vision for positive change.

shaping new ways of doing things and contributing to important conversations in topics that matter most,
